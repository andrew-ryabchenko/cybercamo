LSB steganography, or Least Significant Bit steganography, is a technique for hiding secret data within the least significant bits of each pixel in an image, making the alterations virtually undetectable to the human eye.
Watch this video to better understand what is going on behind the scenes and how the payload data is hidden in an image.
